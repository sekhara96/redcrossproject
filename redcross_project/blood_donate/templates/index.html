Blood Donation

Blood donation is when people give a part of their blood to help others. This donated blood is used to help sick people who need it for surgeries, accidents, or other medical treatments.

Why Donate Blood?


Donating blood is a selfless act that can save lives. Blood donations are essential for various medical procedures, surgeries, and emergencies.
Here are some reasons why you should consider donating blood:
  1. One blood donation can save up to three lives.
  2. Patients undergoing surgeries, cancer treatments, and trauma victims rely on donated blood.
  3. Blood cannot be manufactured; it relies on voluntary donations.

The Blood Donation Process


The blood donation process is simple and safe. Here's what you can expect:
  1. Registration and Health Check: You'll fill out a form and undergo a brief health check.
  2. Donation: A healthcare professional will collect your blood using a sterile needle.
  3. Refreshments: After donating, you'll enjoy refreshments to help your body recover.

Tips for donation
The day before

  • Have an iron-rich diet such as beans, spinach or meat, poultry.
  • Have a proper sleep of at least 8 hours.
  • Include more liquids in your diet.

On the Donating day

  • Do carry your identify identification forms Eg: driver’s license.
  • Drink 2 cups of water before donating blood.
  • Wear a half sleeved shirt so that you can easily roll it up avoid fast food before donating.

After the Donation

  • Reward yourself with a snack as refreshment immediately.
  • Drink more liquids over a period of 24 hours.
  • Remove the bandage after few hours.
  • Don’t do vigorous workout & give your body rest.
